Summary

Ray tracing in graphics is a technique that allows the computer to draw extremely real pictures by using physics! The idea is that the sun's light hits an object and reflects off that object. What we see are the photons hitting our eyes. We use this concept to generate realistic computer graphics by simulating sunlight reflecting off of three dimensional objects and entering an eye. This will allow the computer to simulate shadows, depth, and other interesting things about the object.
